Purpose & Objectives

Knowledge into practiced behavior

A full-day, hands-on simulation targeting the observed gaps: estimation quality, proposal preparation, stakeholder handling with global entities and customers, and negotiation of risks, issues, dependencies and change. All 99 participating DMs have completed the five GDM 2.0 training sessions and two ED rules & guiding principles sessions — this day converts that knowledge into practice.

The Simulation

One day, one live bid, full governance

Phases 1–3 run in parallel for all 20 groups across three development floors. Customer communication is remote-only — a genuine offshore engagement, compressed into a single day.

📋

Phase 1 · Requirement Finalization

Consolidate the RfP into a Requirement Package, run the Open Questions Register, classify RML & UCM, and hold the Reverse Validation Workshop with your customer — remotely.

🧩

Phase 2 · Solution & Approval

Architecture and tech-stack rationale, Technology Risk Assessment, an AI Leverage Plan, and Solution Validation Gate sign-off with the customer in the BrSE role.

🧮

Phase 3 · Estimation, Proposal & BRC

Deliverable-based WBS, risk buffer vs UCM, margin baseline on the Workshop Rate Card — and a live scope-change injection at ~11:30 to test your CR discipline.

🧊

Proposal Freeze · 12:45

Final Technical Proposal plus all 12 artifacts uploaded to the designated folder. Late submissions are flagged to the judges.

🎤

Plenary Presentations

20 groups × 10 minutes hard stop in the Auditorium. Judges score live on a 100-point Microsoft Form while you pitch.

🏆

Recognition

Scores consolidate at 17:30. The Top 3 groups are recognized in the closing session with a leadership message.

Ground Rules

Play it like a real engagement

🔓

Open everything

Open book, open AI, open internet, open laptop — bring every tool you would use with a real customer.

🤖

AI is mandatory

Every proposal carries an AI Leverage Plan showing how AI reduced cost and improved quality — and judges can challenge your claims.

💬

Remote-only customers

Chat and calls only. The customer answers only questions asked properly. If it is not written, it was not agreed.

🔁

No CR, No Execution

A live scope change lands mid-session. Handle it through Change Impact Analysis and a costed CR — absorbing it silently scores against you.
